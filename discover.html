Parks

Zilker Park

Zilker Park is one of Austin's biggest parks, spanning a total of 350 acres. Throughout its many trails are some benches where one can rest, and while resting, admire the beautiful nature, ranging from watching boats paddle across the colorado river to enjoying the shade under some trees.

Butler Metro Park

If you are looking for a park with more of a city vibe, then this is the park for you. There are many park benches next to a playground, surrounded by both foliage and the downtown Austin skyline.

Libraries

UT Libraries

Every single library on the UT Campus is a location filled with many different types of great chairs. The comfort of all of the chairs is enhanced by the quiet environment the chairs are situated in, making the library a natural place for studying and relaxing. Clicking on the above link will allow you to see when each library is open and accessible.

Austin Central Library

Outside of UT, there are many well-established libraries as well, such as the Austin Central Library. Its many windows make the space inside a great spot to hang out and study.

Little Free Library

The Little Free Library is a nonprofit dedicated to community building and expanding book access for everyone. In the vicinity of some of the Little Free Library mini bookshelves are some comfortable park benches, and the general aura is very welcoming.

Chairs on Campus

GDC

If you are looking for modern chairs, the Gates Dell Complex has some very sleek chairs. There is also great ventilation in the indoors space to help create a soothing chill atmosphere perfect for those looking to get away from hot summer days.

WCP

The WCP is a place for students to study and eat food, conveniently located next to the gym and a few student residence halls. There are many rows of benches outside the building, and they are all nice and spread out.
