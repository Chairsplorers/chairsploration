Welcome to Chairsploration, fellow sitting apparatus connoisseur! We know that in your many years of life you've sat your behind in plenty of chairs: desk chairs, office chairs, couches, benches, and you've looked for a lot of chairs: quiet chairs to work, comfy chairs to relax, and outdoor chairs to enjoy some fresh air. We're here to nab all of your sitting expertise and to help you optimize your chair experience.
